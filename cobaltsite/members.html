About the Alliance

The Cobalt Alliance was originally created consisting only the Cobalt Council.
Later on in the Deadfire war, the Cobalt Empire was formed, and the Underworld Kingdoms
joined after the war ended.

The Cobalt Council

The original Cobalt members. Consisting of the 3 clans led by PieRow, Pyrotech and Mixed The Mouse. These are battle-harderned warriors from the Cardigan invasion of Terra.
PieRow's clan members possesses unique Tarik supported by unmatched Extalen equipments, organized into different squads, each commanding their own armies and divisions, with different objectives, making up the perfect war machine.
Pyrotech's clan members are hybrid Draconians with formidable Tarik never seen before. These warriors charge into battle with the spirit of Ancient Drakonia.
Mixed the Mouse's clan is less powerful, but do not disrespect them, when working together, the pose a large threat to anyone in the Alliance's way.

The Cobalt Empire

The Cobalt Empire was formed on the races that joined the Cobalt Alliance during the Deadfire war, along with SP-02 as Headquarters of the Alliance.
These races are: The Illutrons of Illutron and Kryostel; The Neoniers of Neonmere; The Grand Arcanians of Arcana and Arcreign; The Fishrons of The Great Oceans; The Blazketons; The Zenoxians of Zenox and Zenkhan; The Anivians of Anivia; The Mordauns of Mordauh; The Phantomancers of Phantom; The Dreadens of Dreadium and Darkmoor.

The Underworld

  • The Infernars of Inferna, led by PieRow as King and Kristine Electryc as Queen.
  • The Hallowlanders of Hallowland, led by Fallax Pyer as King.
  • The Shadowsorcerers of Shadowshrine, led by Cimmerian as King.
  • The Phaseks of Phasion, led by Bowel Velrus as King and Etila Uern as Queen.
  • The Skeletrons, led by Doney Furer as King.
  • Cursecove.
  • The Raven Tribes.
