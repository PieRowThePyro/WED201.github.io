Where to start?

Instructions

The Lore of the Cobalt universe is extremely long and complicated. If you wish to read and understand the majority of the lore, you will have to start at the more important ones.

Core lore will have the tag "[Core]", while less important will have the tag "[Semi-core]", and things you don't really need to know to understand everything will be tagged "[Additional]". Unfinished work will be tagged "[Unfinished]"

This document will tell you the order of which you should read the lore: What to know?

Recommendations

  • The Universe and Civilization
  • Kingdoms of the Underworld
  • Military Forces of the Underworld
  • Realms of Cobalt [Unfinished]
  • The Deadfire War [Unfinished]
